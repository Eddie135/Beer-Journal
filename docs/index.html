Beer Journal 1.0

Your cellar,
kept local.

An offline-first Android beer journal for bottles, tastings, photos, tags, ratings, and memories.

Download APKView source

Made for daily use

Manage Beer and Tasting records with countries, categories, styles, tags, five-option sensory ratings, search, filters, sorting, and recovery.

Private by default

Records and compressed photos stay on your Android device. There is no account, cloud sync, analytics, or advertising in 1.0.

Back up yourself

Export JSON backups regularly. Uninstalling the app or clearing its data can permanently remove local records and photos.

What comes next

Future candidates include complete photo backups, import conflict reports, CSV export, personalization, and optional synchronization. See the roadmap.
